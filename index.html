Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of pet care services including dog walking, daycare, boarding, home visits, pet sitting, and grooming. Our services are tailored to meet the unique needs of each pet and their owners.

How do I book a service?

Booking a service is easy! You can use our online booking system on our website, give us a call, or send us an email. We'll guide you through the process and help you choose the best service for your pet's needs.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that plans can change. We ask for at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice may be subject to a cancellation fee. Please refer to our detailed policy for more information.

What should I pack for daycare?

For daycare, please bring your dog's food, favorite toy, and any medications they might need. We provide bedding, water bowls, and lots of love! If your dog has any special requirements, please let us know in advance.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking as early as possible, especially for holiday periods. For regular services, 1-2 weeks' notice is usually sufficient. However, we always try our best to accommodate last-minute bookings when possible.
